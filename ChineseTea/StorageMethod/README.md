# Tea Storage Methods (茶叶存储方式)

[中文版](README_cn.md) | [← Back to Home](../../README.md)

## Classification by Storage Form

Tea can be classified by its physical form into **compressed tea** and **loose-leaf tea**.

## Compressed Tea (紧压茶)

Compressed tea is tea that has been pressed into various shapes for easier storage, transportation, and aging. Common forms include:

| Form | Description | Typical Teas |
|------|------------|-------------|
| Cake / Disc (饼茶) | Pressed into flat, round disc shape | Pu'er, Fujian white tea |
| Brick (砖茶) | Pressed into rectangular bricks | Anhua dark tea, Laoqingzhuan |
| Tuo (沱茶) | Pressed into a bowl or nest shape | Yunnan Pu'er, Xiaguan tuo |
| Other shapes | Various sizes for portability | Mini cakes, dragon balls, etc. |

Compression serves several purposes:
- **Facilitates long-term aging** — Pu'er and white tea are commonly compressed into cakes to allow slow transformation over years
- **Easier storage and transport** — Compact shapes take less space
- **Convenient portioning** — Small compressed forms are easy to break off and brew

## Loose-Leaf Tea (散茶)

Loose-leaf tea is stored in its natural, uncompressed form. Most green, yellow, and oolong teas are sold as loose-leaf. Proper storage for loose-leaf tea:

- **Airtight containers** — Keep away from air to prevent oxidation
- **Cool, dark place** — Avoid heat and sunlight
- **Away from odors** — Tea absorbs nearby scents easily
- **Low humidity** — Moisture causes mold and deterioration
- **Refrigeration** — Recommended for delicate green teas (sealed to prevent moisture absorption)

## Storage Tips by Tea Type

| Tea Type | Best Storage | Shelf Life |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Green Tea | Sealed, refrigerated | 6–18 months |
| White Tea | Dry, ventilated; can age | Years to decades |
| Yellow Tea | Sealed, cool place | 6–12 months |
| Oolong Tea | Sealed; roasted types are more stable | 1–3 years (light); years (roasted) |
| Black Tea | Sealed, room temperature | 1–3 years |
| Dark Tea | Dry, ventilated; improves with age | Decades |
